Student Wellness Hub

Your mental health, safety, and wellbeing matters at NWU

🧠 Mental Health Support

NWU offers confidential counselling services for stress, anxiety, depression, and personal challenges. Students can book private sessions with trained professionals.

📚 Academic Stress Management

Learn how to manage workload, prepare for exams, and balance academic pressure through workshops and peer support programmes.

🤝 Peer Support System

Connect with trained student mentors who help guide first-years and returning students through university life challenges.

🚨 Emergency Support

Immediate help is available through NWU campus security and emergency wellness services for urgent student situations.

Academic tutoring is available through student academic support.

Students can apply through NSFAS or university bursary offices.
